IT Lifecycle & Validation

Efficiency, reliability, and compliance.

The System Development Lifecycle (SDLC) and the Computer System Validation (CSV) programs in place within your IT Organization are the backbone of smooth and efficient IT projects. The SDLC will inform the processes for defining, implementing, and managing regulated and non-regulated enterprise systems from inception to retirement.

The CSV will guide your organization through the processes for providing evidence that your GxP IT systems operate and perform as intended.

How We Help

If you are a "young" IT Organization and need help to define your SDLC and CSV, or if you have an outdated or overly burdensome set of system lifecycle and validation procedures, StratusG can help to ensure that your future state SDLC and CSV are right-sized and compliant with current best practices.

  • SDLC program definition and optimization
  • Computer System Validation (CSV) program development
  • Compliance with current GxP best practices
  • Right-sizing validation procedures for your organization
  • System lifecycle management from inception to retirement

Immunotherapy, CAR-T & Gene Therapy

Chain of Identity & Chain of Custody solutions for autologous therapies.

StratusG has worked with a multitude of customers, including with contract drug manufacturing organizations (CDMO) often hired by immunotherapy and Gene Therapy companies to manufacture drug product.

StratusG understands the tremendous importance of managing and evidencing the end-to-end chain of identity (COI) and chain of custody (COC) of every single unique batch or lot of autologous drug product.

Our Capabilities

  • Implementation of leading commercial off-the-shelf COI/COC software products
  • Custom COI/COC solution development for clinical trials and commercial products
  • Partnership with CDMO organizations for manufacturing process support
  • Refinement of COI/COC product offerings for the immunotherapy sector
  • End-to-end chain management from clinical trials through commercial products

StratusG has also worked with the two leading COI/COC software product providers to help them refine their product offerings to be more applicable to the rapidly growing immunotherapy sector.
